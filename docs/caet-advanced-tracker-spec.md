## Evaluation Model

### Shop-Based (Primary Path)
- Shop designates an authorized evaluator
- Evaluator must complete train-the-trainer qualification
- Evaluator signs off each task as technician demonstrates competency
- All sign-offs tracked in database with evaluator name, date, company
- AEA maintains registry of qualified evaluators

### Alternative Paths
- **AEA HQ Classes** ‚Äî Instructor signs off tasks covered in class ‚Üí uploaded to tracker
- **AEA Regional Classes** ‚Äî Same model (available fall 2026+)
- **OEM Training** (Garmin, Collins, etc.) ‚Äî Could count toward practical (mapping TBD)
- **Example:** Complete AEA pitot-static class ‚Üí signed off on pitot-static category

### Rollout Plan (Scott Samuelson's framework)
1. **Phase A:** Perfect at AEA internally (committee beta tests)
2. **Phase B:** Offer at AEA regionals
3. **Phase C:** Shops apply to become authorized evaluation centers

## CAET Advanced Package (Bundled Product ‚Äî Feb 18)

**Core Insight:** The tracking tool isn't a standalone product. Nobody buys software. They buy a certification path. The tool is how it gets delivered.

### What the Shop Buys (One Price)
- CAET Advanced certification path (Practical ‚Üí Written ‚Üí Oral)
- Training content / study materials
- Tracking tool access (PQS progress, evidence uploads, evaluator sign-offs)
- Micro training library (QR-scannable bench reference modules)
- Written exam through USI
- Oral board evaluation

### How It Gets Funded
Shops don't pay out of pocket if they stack correctly:
1. **Funding Finder** ‚Üí identifies available money (state tax credits, apprenticeship reimbursement)

3. **SkillBridge** ‚Üí military candidates' training is DoD-funded
4. **COOL** ‚Üí Navy/Marine credentialing funds
5. **State tax credits** ‚Üí 22 states have workforce training credits

## RTI Hour Logging

All training activities must log Related Technical Instruction hours for DOL apprenticeship compliance.

### What Counts as RTI
- CAET study guide modules (self-paced)
- QR micro training modules (video pipeline content)
- AEA HQ classes
- AEA regional classes
- OEM training (Garmin, Collins, etc.)
- Online coursework

### Tracking Requirements
- Time-stamped start/stop per module
- Module completion verification (quiz pass, minimum time threshold)
- Maps to apprenticeship phase (1-4) and competency domain
- Exportable for DOL compliance reporting
- 586 total RTI hours across program (150/150/100/100 + 86 employer-provided)

---

## OJT Hour Tracking

Separate from RTI. On-the-Job Training hours tracked by employer.

### What Gets Tracked
- Hours worked on specific competency areas
- Supervisor attestation
- Competency sign-offs (PQS tasks double as OJT evidence for apprenticeship shops)
- Wage progression documentation

### Integration with PQS
- PQS practical task sign-offs in the tracker serve BOTH purposes:
  - CAET Advanced certification requirement
  - Apprenticeship OJT competency evidence
- One action by the evaluator, two compliance paths satisfied

## Micro Training / QR Code System (POST-CONVENTION)

### Concept
QR codes at shop workstations ‚Üí scan ‚Üí instant micro training module on phone.

### Content Pipeline
1. Danny (or any shop) shoots short video of bench task
2. AI pipeline: download ‚Üí transcribe ‚Üí extract key frames ‚Üí generate draft module
3. **Nick reviews and approves** (human-in-the-loop, mandatory)
4. Published to training library, QR code generated
5. Module completion logs RTI hours in tracker

### Rules
- Content ONLY from source video ‚Äî no AI-invented material
- No editorializing on tools, brands, or methods
- AEA cannot endorse specific products or criticize alternatives
- Every module is a DRAFT until Nick approves
